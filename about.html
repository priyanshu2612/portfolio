Priyanshu Who?

Hoii! I'm Priyanshu Barnwal, a Product and Motion Designer from IIT Guwahati. Although a Chemical Engineer by qualification, I'm a Product Designer by passion. My love for making user-centric products began in my sophomore year and ever-since I find myself entangled in crafting experiences and solutions that make the lives of people easier. I have a knack for problem solving.

🙏 My experiences

-  Recently, at TechnoCube I served as a UX intern in December, 2019 and then as a remote- Product Designer for 5 months in 2020
-  In the last one and a half years, I had an awesome experience to work with a talented pool of designers, founders and managers. Through my internships in various companies I gained a lot of experience and knowledge in dealing with real projects.
-  In college, I took an active part in clubs, served as a Design Head at a symposium and a lot more. Cultural activites like dance, drama, etc. equally interested me.

It all started 1 year back with giving wings to my idea of a social media based app to revolutionize the learning structure after which I believe my dedication helped me in learning various aspects of UI and UX. I managed to launch this app with two of my friends.
